Our Workspace

UL Technology Solutions Pvt Ltd, has its main delivery centre located in Kozhikode, Kerala. Situated inside UL Cyberpark, the centre is nestled within nature, surrounded by lush greeneries - a hallmark that the State is widely known for. The blue sky, the greens all around, occasional high rises and a pollution free environment outline the culture that has been inculcated in the company, influenced by the tradition and heritage fostered by its parent, ULCCS, over a period of close to a hundred years.

At the heart of this culture lies an unadulterated spirit of empathy, humility and unconditional collaboration. Our Leaders emanate these human values each day which tickles down to the rest of the organisation every moment.

Our Culture

We have built an organisation that is client focused, performance-driven, innovation-centric and employee-oriented. We go out of the way to put ourselves in Client’s shoes to build solutions that would make them pioneers in their respective industries.
